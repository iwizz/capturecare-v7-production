# Master Calendar - Quick Reference Guide

## üéØ Quick Actions

### Viewing Appointments
- **Month View**: Colored rectangles show appointments (blue) and availability (green)
- **Week View**: Time grid shows appointments as badges with patient names
- **Day View**: Practitioner columns show detailed appointment information

### Booking Appointments
1. **From Availability** (Green slots):
   - **Month**: Click green rectangle ‚Üí Modal opens with practitioner pre-selected
   - **Week**: Click green slot ‚Üí Modal opens with date/time pre-selected
   - **Day**: Click green slot ‚Üí Modal opens with practitioner/date/time pre-selected
   
2. **From "New Appointment" Button**:
   - Click button in top-right corner
   - Fill in all details manually

### Moving Appointments
- **Drag and Drop**:
  - **Month**: Drag appointment to different day (keeps same time)
  - **Week**: Drag appointment to different day/time
  - **Day**: Drag appointment to different practitioner/time
  
- **Visual Feedback**:
  - Appointment becomes semi-transparent while dragging
  - Drop zones highlight in blue
  - Success notification appears after drop

### Viewing Appointment Details
- **Click any appointment** (blue rectangle/badge)
- Side drawer opens with full details
- Can edit or delete from drawer

## üîß Special Features

### Block Mode
1. Toggle "Block Mode" checkbox
2. Click available slots to mark as blocked
3. Click blocked slots to unblock
4. Works in Week and Day views

### Practitioner Filter
1. Click practitioner badges at top
2. Green checkmark = active (showing)
3. Faded = inactive (hidden)
4. Click to toggle on/off

### View Navigation
- **Month ‚Üí Week**: Click any day in month view
- **Week ‚Üí Day**: Switch to Day view and navigate to date
- **Today**: Click "Today" button to jump to current date

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

1. **Appointments must have**:
   - Patient
   - Practitioner
   - Date & Time
   - Duration
   - Title

2. **Drag and Drop**:
   - Only works for existing appointments
   - Can't drag to blocked slots
   - Can't drag to unavailable times

3. **Availability**:
   - Green slots = practitioner is available
   - Multiple practitioners can be available at same time
   - Click any available practitioner to book

4. **Conflicts**:
   - System prevents double-booking
   - Warning appears if conflict detected
   - Can override if needed

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "No appointments showing"
- Check practitioner filter (make sure some are selected)
- Verify you're looking at correct date range
- Refresh the page

### "Can't drag appointment"
- Make sure appointment has valid ID
- Check that drop zone is available (green)
- Try clicking to edit instead

### "Available slots not showing"
- Verify practitioner has availability patterns set
- Check for company-wide blocks/holidays
- Ensure practitioner is active

### "Modal won't open"
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors
- Try different browser
