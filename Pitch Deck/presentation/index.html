What We've Built

CaptureCare V7 is a comprehensive healthcare management platform that bridges the gap between patient data, clinical practice, and artificial intelligence.

Mission

To help medical practices manage patients, track health data, schedule appointments, and provide personalized health insights using advanced AI.

For Practitioners

Streamlined workflow, AI insights, and automated admin tasks.

For Patients

Personalized care, easy scheduling, and clear health reports.

Core Features

Patient Management

  • Complete patient registration & profiles
  • Medical history & clinical notes
  • Unified communication log (Email, SMS, Voice)
  • Billing & Invoice tracking

Health Data Integration

  • Withings device integration (Scales, BP)
  • Auto-sync: Weight, HR, BP, Sleep
  • Interactive charts & trend analysis
  • Smart target ranges based on age/condition

AI & Scheduling

AI-Powered Analysis

  • OpenAI GPT-4 generated health reports
  • Trend analysis & recommendations
  • Auto-generated SOAP notes
  • HeyGen AI avatar video explanations

Smart Scheduling

  • Interactive calendar (Month/Week/Day)
  • Multi-practitioner support
  • Drag-and-drop rescheduling
  • Auto-SMS notifications on change

Operations & Integration

Practice Mgmt

Cliniko integration for 2-way sync of patient data and treatment notes.

Communication

Twilio powered SMS, Voice, and Video consultations with transcription.

Billing

Stripe integration for invoices, payments, and recurring subscriptions.

Data & Reporting

Secure storage, Google Sheets export for analytics, and comprehensive system logging.

Security Architecture

Authentication

  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Secure HttpOnly session cookies
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
  • Auto-logout on inactivity

Data Protection

  • Secret Manager for API keys
  • SQL Injection protection (ORM)
  • OAuth2 for device authorization
  • Environment isolation (Dev/Prod)
